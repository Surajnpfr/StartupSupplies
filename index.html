Founded in early 2026, Startup Supplies is based in Nepal to support importers and exporters with reliable and cost-effective logistics solutions. Our primary operations are in India, with active service availability across key China trade routes through a trusted partner network.

Our focus is simple: move your cargo efficiently, communicate clearly, and solve problems quickly - every time.
